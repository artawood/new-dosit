About Dosit Thai Cuisine

Where tradition, craftsmanship, and hospitality come together.

Our Story

Since our opening in 2009, we have strived to deliver the best quality in our food and our service. "Dosit" in Thai means heavenly, and we have worked to translate that meaning into a unique experience for our restaurant guest. Located in the quiet wilderness of Walnut Creek, you will find peace and tranquility while enjoying our traditional Thai cuisine made from the freshest ingredients.

Meet Our Chef

Jakkrid Laongsuwan, our chef, had been in the restaurant industry for almost 15 years. He had worked as executive chef in many 5 star resorts in Thailand, including the famous Dusit Thani Hotel and Resort in Bangkok, the capital city. Mr. Laongsuwan had spent 10 years perfecting the balance between the color and taste in his creations. His exemplary culinary experience will be sure to take your taste buds on a heavenly ride.
